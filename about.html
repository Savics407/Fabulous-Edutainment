Get to Know Us

Fabulous Edutainment is a company fuelled by an immeasurable passion to restructure the education and entertainment sectors in Africa through creation of diverse opportunities for purposeful talents.

Our Vision

To Restructure the educational and entertainment sectors in Africa through talents.

Our Mission

To mine, refine, manage and project purposeful talents in youths for global impact.

It is worthy of note that about 70% of African youths spend lots of money in all kinds of schools and never refined their gifts. Most often, their decision to choose a certain area of study is motivated more by economic potentials than personal fulfillment. So, they either graduate then get stock looking for a job or finds a job that gives them the money but still grapple with the complexity of understanding the purpose for their lives.

These youths go through life not knowing who they are and where they are going. They tend to flow along with or without finances feeling unfulfilled and unsatisfied. They miss opportunities because of ignorance of what to do in alignment to their purpose.

F.E through her talent school gives solution to the aforementioned core issue prevalent amongst our youths thus working with experts across various distributions of talents in a bit to educate and coach desirous youth on their specific area of talent and gifting as well create diverse opportunities to project them to the spotlight.


Our sector of focus is education and entertainment.Fabulous edutainment has her main branch in Enugu State, Nigeria. Her team structures includes; Chief Operation Officer, Financial Manager, Talent Directors, Event Manager,Market Strategist, Technical Personnels.

Other teams of necessary importance include visual Hyping and Promotion team whose duty is to make awesome videos using the various talent sets in the company in a bit to render first of its kind and all packed advertisement and promotion for the company and other company/brands as required by the contracting party.
